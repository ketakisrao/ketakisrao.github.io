LiveReach Media

Analytics based solution for out of home advertising

Context

In the world of out of home advertising, there are two main stakeholders, Advertisers - who want to run ad campaigns and Publishers - who maintain the digital inventory on which ads can run (ex: billboards, tv displays, etc). LiveReach media has a platform to connect these two entities and provide them with analytics on these ad campaigns.

Problem

The initial inventory onboarding process for Publishers was lengthy and complicated which is why a lot of LRM’s time & effort was being spent into helping customers get onto the system instead of helping them with the analytics data of their ad campaigns.

Solution

Redesigning the existing interface to make the onboarding process faster and smoother.

Impact

The design changes increased the average onboarding time by 33% and resulted in the conversion of two major clients to paid customers after successful pilots.

Design Decisions

Integrated external search for google maps to automatically add a display pin on the map so that publishers don’t have to click on the map or waste time time in pin pointing the right location to do so.

Integrated image editing tools such as crop, rotate & zoom into the add site process so that users don’t have to leave the platform to do so or entirely abandon the process because they don’t have software to do so.

A site comprised of three or more devices which needed to be pinned one at a time by the user. The process was modified such that a user can pin multiple devices in a single sitting.

Feature Design

Apart from just being a platform to schedule ads campaigns, LiveReach Media also provides data metrics such as number of views, gender ratio, dwell time, etc for these out-of-home advertisements. Here are a few features that I’ve designed and developed.

Live dashboard which updates values such as headcount and wait in real time (using websockets). This helps in monitoring the data and making adjustments in real time.

Live tracking of people in an area(site) to be able to determine trends and patterns in movement, congregation points, flow, etc.
